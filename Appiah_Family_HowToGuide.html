Common questions

FAQs

Can I change my prediction after saving?
Yes — you can change it as many times as you like after saving, right up until you tap 🔒 Lock. Once locked, it cannot be changed.
What if I forget to lock before kick-off?
Once the admin closes a round, predictions are frozen. If you haven't locked in time, your saved prediction still counts — but it's best to lock as soon as you're sure.
Do I need to create an account?
No! Just open the file, tap Player, and type your name. That's it — no passwords, no accounts needed.
Will my predictions be saved if I close the app?
Yes — as long as you tapped Save (and ideally Lock), your predictions are stored in the cloud. Open the app again on any device and they'll be there.
How do I know when a new round opens?
If you added your email or WhatsApp number when signing up, you'll get a notification. You'll also see a pop-up the next time you open the app.
Where can I see the leaderboard?
The admin shares leaderboard updates via WhatsApp or email after each round. You can also see your own score in the 🏆 My Tally tab anytime.
